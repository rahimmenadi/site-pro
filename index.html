🚶‍♂️ Walkability Perception: Annotated Snapshots

📍 Walk 1 – Rue Gaston Viel

📝 During this walk, I noticed a few people, especially students from nearby medical schools. There were some storefronts, but most of them seemed inactive, which made the street feel a bit empty. I also saw some vegetation and interesting urban elements, like big statues or artistic structures, which added to the identity of the place. If the walk started earlier — from the middle of Allée de l'Île Gloriette — it could include more active storefronts and variety

📍 Walk 2 –2 Pl. Albert Camus

📝 I did this walk around 2:30 PM, just after lunch. The street had most of the typical features — storefronts, sidewalks, and a good layout — but the atmosphere felt quite empty. One thing that stood out was that the island streets seemed unusually inactive, with very few people around. It’s a pleasant area, but it’s hard to know how active it really gets at other times of the day.

📍 Walk 3 –21 Rue Léon Durocher

📝This walk also took place on an island, but it felt more active compared to the previous one. I did it around 12 PM, and I saw more people using the space. The area seemed lively and had everything you’d expect: bikes, sidewalks, shops, and movement. It gave a good impression of a functional and engaging street, although I’m not sure if it stays this active at other times of the day.

📍 Walk 4 – Quai de la Fosse

📝 This part of the walk took place on a very active street with plenty of movement and urban elements. I walked on the left sidewalk, which felt functional but a bit dry — there was no visible vegetation or trees on this side. Even though the street was busy and lively, the lack of greenery made this part feel a little less comfortable.

📍 Walk 5 – 74 Quai de la Fosse

📝 This second part of the street continued to be very active and well-used, but this time I was on the right sidewalk. One thing that stood out was the presence of trees and vegetation, which made the space feel more pleasant and walkable. It gave a stronger sense of comfort and visual quality compared to the other side.
